20m Homebrew Transceiver

This project is a compact homebrew QRP transceiver designed for portable HF operation. It focuses on low power consumption and simplicity, ideal for field activations like SOTA.

Technical Specifications

  • Band: 20m (14 MHz)
  • Output Power: approx. 5 W
  • Modes: CW / Digital
  • Custom RF design
  • Optimized for portable operation

Project Overview

The design emphasizes simplicity and efficiency. RF stages are optimized for stable operation with minimal components. The transceiver is intended for portable field use.

Development Notes

Future updates may include additional filtering, improved frequency stability, and enclosure integration.
